About

This is "1000 Blank White Cards" for the internet.

Playing the Original Game is hard. Nowadays getting pens, paper, and people in a room together is almost impossible. But don't despair, you've got the power of digital cards right in your pocket - anywhere, anytime!

This is a social party game where you make the rules. Best played with friends on videocall or IRL - is that's even possible???

Open up a custom room - or use the totally crowdsourced global deck - to start playing right away! Think of a title, description, and draw a picture - just like writing on cardstock - let your imagination run wild!

You can also save and re-use custom decks. Relive the memories - view and edit your collection offline for next time. There's even a "suck box" system for the global deck so the latest and greatest cards appear more often!

Go get your squad together and play! I know you'll have fun :)
